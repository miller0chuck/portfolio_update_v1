Creativity & Intuition

Motivation

My life as an artist could be described as either erratic or andventurous. I spent my high school years preparing myself for a career of science and mathematics but I secretly admired something more. I finally went with my intuition (and against parental advice) to get my art degree. In college I went through a whirlwind of practices ranging from sculpture to photography to bookmaking. For the past 4 years I've spent time falling in love with the underrated floral arts and found inspiration in horticulture. Now I'm returning to mathematics and science with new eyes in the field of Web Development. Ya, I'd say it's an adventure. ❧
